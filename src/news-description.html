European Esports Federation (EEF) Concludes Successful General Assembly in Poland

The International Esports Federation announces that the European Continental Body, European Esports Federation (EEF), has concluded its General Assembly held on April 27th, 2024, in Poland, marking an important step forward in the organization’s leadership and strategic direction. The assembly involved the elections of the EEF President, new Board Members, and Audit Committee members, all aimed at guiding the EEF into its next phase of growth and development.

Representatives from all European Esports federations gathered to participate in this event, reflecting the united commitment to advancing Esports across the continent.

The newly elected EEF President, Mr. Andrzej Kraśnicki (Poland), brings a vision and leadership that will shape the future of Esports in Europe, promoting the values and aspirations of the federation.
